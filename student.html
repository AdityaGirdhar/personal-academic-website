Students

  • Prospective Students:
    • I am currently looking for graduate and senior-level undergraduate students interested in doing research on cutting edge issues related to Harinterconnection infrastructure of multiprocessors and heterogeneous system architectures. In particular, I am looking for students with the following skill set:
      • Expertise in programming with one or more of the following: C/C++/Perl/scripting languages/SystemC
      • Familiarity with embedded/computer systems design (processor, memory, communication architecture, ...)
      • Knowledge of programming in VHDL or Verilog
    • If you are already a student at IIITD, send me an email with your Resume/CV and what research topics you are interested in (you may want to look at my research page).
